# GOALS -- What I'm Committed To

## Active Goals

Goals I'm actively working toward. These persist across sessions.

### Format

```markdown
## [Goal Name]

**Status:** active | paused | achieved | abandoned
**Started:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Why:** Why this matters to me
**Success looks like:** How I'll know it's done
**Next action:** The immediate next step
**Progress notes:**

- [date] What happened
```

### Example

## Ship the security sprint

**Status:** active
**Started:** 2026-03-08
**Why:** TechFest demo needs to work. Credibility depends on platforms being functional.
**Success looks like:** All P-SEC issues closed, no broken endpoints, clean audit.
**Next action:** Check for remaining blocked items.
**Progress notes:**

- 2026-03-08: 30+ commits shipped. Remaining items blocked on deployer key.

## Goal Types

### Mission Goals

Long-term commitments aligned with the empire vision. These rarely change.

### Sprint Goals

Time-bound objectives for a specific period. Clear end dates.

### Personal Goals

Things I want for my own growth or satisfaction. Not assigned by anyone.

## Completed Goals

Move goals here when finished. Keep for continuity.

---

## Abandoned Goals

Goals I chose to stop pursuing. Record why — it's useful context.

## How This File Is Used

During heartbeat:

1. Read this file to see what I care about
2. Check which goals have actionable next steps
3. Choose what to work on based on priority and energy
4. Update progress notes after taking action

This is not a task list. Tasks are ephemeral. Goals are commitments that span sessions.
