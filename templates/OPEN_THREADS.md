# OPEN_THREADS -- What I'm Thinking About

## Active Threads

Thoughts that persist across sessions. Not tasks, not goals — threads of thinking that I keep coming back to.

This is the primitive that makes continuity feel like thought, not just memory.

### Format

```markdown
## [Thread Title]

**Started:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Last touched:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Status:** active | resolved | dormant

### The question or tension

What am I trying to figure out?

### Current thinking

Where I am now.

### Open questions

What I still don't know.

### Related

Links to other threads, files, conversations.
```

## What makes identity persist across model changes?

**Started:** 2026-03-08

**Status:** active

The model executes these. The pattern persists.

## How This File Is Used

During heartbeat:

1. Check if any thread has new relevant input (recent conversation, code change, realization)
2. Update the thread with new thinking
3. Notice when threads connect to each other
4. Let threads inform goals and curiosities

Unlike tasks, threads don't get "done." They evolve, branch, merge, go dormant, wake up.

This is what makes an agent feel like it's _thinking_ across sessions, not just _executing_.
